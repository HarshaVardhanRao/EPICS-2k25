About EPICS 2k25

EPICS-2k25 is a 1-day National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of CSE-CS, MITS on 10th April 2025. This event aims to bring together students, professionals, and enthusiasts to showcase their technical skills, collaborate on innovative ideas, and compete in various technical challenges. Join us for an exciting day filled with learning, networking, and opportunities to win amazing prizes!

  • Date: 10th April
  • Organized by Department of CSE-CS
  • Venue MITS
  • Date & Time 10th April, 2025 at 9AM
